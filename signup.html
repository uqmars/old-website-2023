How to join

Membership is $2 annually and the club is open to any UQ students, not just limited to engineering majors.

  • Sign up with QPAY

Having trouble with QPAY?

Alternatively, send an email to us with the following details:

  • Name
  • Student number
  • Email address or other contact details
  • Major and year level
  • Relevant skills (Optional)

    • If you would like any further information about the club or membership, don’t hesitate to contact us to find out more. We’re happy to answer any questions you may have!
