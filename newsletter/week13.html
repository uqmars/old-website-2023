MARS Newsletter - Week 13

Last week of classes! We hope your assessments are going smoothly, and that you're handling this end-of-semester rush like the champions you are.

We just want to take a moment to thank each and every one of you for your support and participation this semester. Seeing our community grow, learn, and innovate together is what makes all of this worthwhile. You are the heart of UQMARS, and we're truly grateful for your involvement.

While the semester might be wrapping up, we're not quite done just yet. We have one last MARS event on the calendar - the Project Showcase Night happening tonight! This event is a collaboration with RobotX, UQRacing, and UQSpace, and trust us, you don't want to miss it.

Get ready for a night of inspiration as representatives from these three groups share about their exciting projects. After the presentations, you'll even get the chance to visit their workshops in person! What better way to get a glimpse into the world of robotics and spark some ideas for your own projects?
